Best of Makoto Shinkai's Movies According to Michael

My Top 3 Anime Movie of All Time

Your Name

Unique and Intriguing Concept:
"Your Name" introduces a compelling concept of body swapping between two characters, Taki and Mitsuha, who live in different places and times. The movie beautifully explores their connection, blending elements of romance, fantasy, and drama in a thought-provoking manner.

Weathering With You

Emotional Depth:
"Weathering with You" delves into complex emotions and tackles the challenges faced by the characters. It explores the desire to change one's circumstances, the weight of responsibility, and the bittersweet nature of love. The emotional depth of the story allows viewers to connect with the characters and become emotionally invested in their journey.

Suzume no Tojimari

The film gives a hard-hitting message to the audience trying to reassure them that even if at times there seems to be no hope, they need to forgive themselves and give their inner child the chance to heal.
